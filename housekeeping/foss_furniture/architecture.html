Architecture of the Home:

    Public Quarters
  • Entrance Hall — Where guests remove shoes and formally enter the private home-space. Shoe-cabinet. Sidetable.
  • Drawing Room — Guests entertaining room. Couches and tea. 3-seat sofa, 2-seat, 1-seat sofa set. Coffee table.
  • Living Room — Tenants shared living space. Television, lounge chair, sofa and family computer. Lounge chair (2). Floor cushions (4). Low Table. Day bench.
  • Dining Room — Seating, plates and cutlery for 6. Dining Table. 6 straight-back dining chairs.
  • Kitchen — Visible from Dining Room. Fridge, Gas stove-top, French Press, Rice cooker. 4 Bar stools.
  • Guest Bathroom — Non-personal bathroom. Carries basic necessities & medicine kit.
    Entertainment
  • Home Theater — Dedicated room for film-watching. Projector, sound system & chairs. 4-seat sofa. Love sofa. 6 fold-out seating.
  • Patio — Outdoor space. Chairs, BBQ. Outdoor bench. 4 side-chairs. Outdoor table. Extra fold-out chairs.
  • Pool — For exercise and entertainment.
