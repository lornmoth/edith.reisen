Site Structure

  • MAIN INDEX listing CATEGORIES listing nested SUBTOPICS
  • CATEGORY shows all SUBTOPICS with nested ARTICLES or BOOKS
  • ARTICLES contain POSTS consisting of TOC, BACKGROUND, PRINCIPLES, CHAPTERS, APPENDIX
    • Top of ARTICLE has a DIRECTORY HEADING listing CATEGORY:SUBTOPIC leading back to SUBTOPIC
    • Each POST has h2 POST TITLE, h3 SUBCHAPTERS, PARAGRAPHS, LISTS
    • Between each POST are SUBFOOTERS directing back to TOC
    • Clicking on HEADER redirects A HREF to direct # URL
  • BOOKS contain POSTS consisting of TOC, BACKGROUND, PRINCIPLES, CHAPTERS, APPENDIX
    • BOOKS have tight MARGINS and a container drawn around all internal POSTS
  • APPENDIX and TOC have small MARGINS
  • Above APPENDIX is a HR
  • Below TOC is a HR and no SUBFOOTER
  • APPENDIX features a secondary TOC
  • The final POST of every APPENDIX is FURTHER READING linking to (1) RELATED ARTICLES (2) EXTERNAL REFERENCES
